My strengths and methods

You need to speak to me for more info, but to give an overview...

Prototyping

Hands-on experience with Paper Prototyping, Rapid Prototyping, Storyboards, Wire-frames and Mock-up design, and Low/High - fidelity prototyping

Visual Design/Development

Visual elements design using Photoshop and Adobe Creative Suite, High-fidelity interactive design using AXURE and HTML 5, CSS3, Javascript technologies.

Information Architecture

Structural design of shared information environments; Hierarchical model design, Use-case diagrams and User flow diagramming.

Usability and Heuristic Evaluation

Execution of usability and heuristic evaluation of the design/prototype, including post-processing/analysis/reporting during various phases of development.

Formative and Evaluative Research

Conduct formative research by administering appropriate techniques such as focus group interview, field interview, ethnography, contextual inquiry, affinity diagramming. I am strong proponent of mixed methods approach.

Product Management

Strong attention to details and nuances, facilitate Design Thinking sessions, Orchestrate product development strategy, Market analysis, Requirement and KPI analysis
